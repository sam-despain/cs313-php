About me

Hello. I am Samuel Despain, and I am a resident of sunny Gilbert, Arizona. I have not lived there my whole life, though. I had the privilege of living in different climates in different parts of the world.

I was born in Provo, Utah, when my parents were students at BYU. During the first part of my childhood, my family had moved around between Arizona, Colorado, and even Spain.

We settled in Colorado until the end of my freshman year of high school, after which we moved to Arizona where the family currently resides.

Mere weeks after graduating high school, I embarked on a full-time proselyting mission. I spent the first twelve weeks in the Arizona Tempe Mission, after which I was called to the Oregon Eugene Mission.

Upon returning home, I began attending BYU-Idaho. A couple of years into college, I began dating a lovely, virtuous young woman named Edith. When I proposed to her, she agreed to be my bride.

My family

My famiy consists of my father and mother, myself, my younger brothers Isaac and Ethan, and my little sister Elena.

Both my parents served in the Spain Bilbao Mission and studied at BYU. My mother earned a master's degree at Colorado State University.

Isaac served in the Germany Berlin Mission, is currently studying at BYU-Idaho, and is married to a wonderful red-head named Bailey.

Ethan recently returned from the Washington Kennewick Mission and is now studying at BYU.

Elena is the only child in our family to have been home-schooled. She became a Young Woman at the start of the Church's new youth program.
